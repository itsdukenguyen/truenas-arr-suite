## Recommended Naming Scheme

**Artist Folder**: `{Artist CleanName}`  
**Album Folder**: `{Artist CleanName}/{Album Title} ({Release Year})`

**Track Format:**

**Example:**
- `The Beatles - Abbey Road (1969) - 01 - Come Together [FLAC]`
